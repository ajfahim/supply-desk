What is Pipe Schedule?
A pipe schedule (SCH) is a measure of a steel pipe's nominal wall thickness.
Metric Rules-of-Thumb
1 mm = about 1/25 inch = thickness of a dime
25 mm = about 1 inch (1" = 25.4 mm)
300 mm = about 1 foot (12" = 304.8 mm)
1 m¬≤ = roughly 10 sq. ft. (1 m¬≤ = 10.76 sq. ft.)
1 L = about 1 quart (1 L = 1.06 qt.)
1 kg = about 2.2 pounds
100 kg = 1 Mg = 1 metric ton = about 2200 lbs.
